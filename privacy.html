Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2024

Brand: Asplix

Contact: Asplixapp@gmail.com

Asplix ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide while using our mobile application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our App:

a. User Information

  • Email address (if you sign in or subscribe)
  • App User ID (automatically generated or manually assigned)
  • Subscription status

b. Receipt & Expense Data

  • Uploaded receipt images (via camera or gallery)
  • Manually entered expense data (e.g., item name, amount, participants)
  • Splitting and labeling metadata

c. Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system version, device language
  • Crash reports and diagnostic logs
  • Usage analytics (e.g., screen views, feature engagement)

d. Third-Party Services

We integrate with third-party tools that may collect data as part of their service:

  • RevenueCat (for subscription tracking)
  • Firebase (for analytics and crash reporting)
  • OCR APIs (for receipt text recognition)

Each of these services complies with industry-standard data protection practices. Their data policies are available on their respective websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core features (e.g., receipt scanning, expense splitting)
  • Process subscription payments and manage access to paid features
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and enhance user experience
  • Respond to user support requests
  • Send important updates or account-related messages

We do not use your data for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties.

3. Subscription Information

Asplix offers a monthly subscription of $1.99 USD, which unlocks premium features. Payments and renewals are handled securely through RevenueCat and the App Store or Google Play. We do not store payment information directly.

4. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access or Update Data: You can update your receipt records or contact us to correct stored personal data.
  • Delete Your Account: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data via email.
  • Opt Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics tracking in your device settings (for Firebase-based tracking).
  • Children's Privacy: The App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), secure storage, and restricted internal access. However, no system is completely immune to breaches.

7. International Users

Your data may be processed or stored in servers outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer in accordance with this policy.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the date at the top and notify users through the App or email, if appropriate.
